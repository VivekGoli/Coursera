Indian Premier League

RCB




Royal Challengers Bangalore are a franchise cricket team based in Bangalore, Karnataka, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). It was founded in 2008 by United Spirits and named after the company's liquor brand Royal Challenge.
Key players: Virat, FAF duPlessis, Maxwell, Dinesh Karthik, Josh, Harshal

RR




Rajasthan Royals are a franchise cricket team based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, that plays in the Indian Premier League. Founded in 2008 as one of the initial eight IPL franchises. The team won the inaugural edition of the IPL under the captaincy of Shane Warne. This team has a very big fan base.
Key players: Buttler, Samson, Hetmeyer, Ashwin, Chahal

SRH




Sun Risers Hyderabad are a franchise cricket team based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, that plays in the Indian Premier League. The franchise is owned by Kalanithi Maran of the SUN Group and was founded in 2012 after the Hyderabad-based Deccan Chargers were terminated by the IPL.
Key players: Kane, Rahul Thripati, Markram, Bhuvi, Umran
